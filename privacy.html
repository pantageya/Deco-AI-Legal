Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Deco collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app and related services.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to AI Deco, including mobile app features for uploading photos, writing prompts, generating interior design images, saving results, managing subscriptions, and contacting support.

The data processed depends on the features you use, permissions you grant, your device, and the services required to provide the requested feature.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Photos, images, prompts, room selections, style choices, color selections, material selections, and other creative inputs you submit.
  • Support emails, feedback, bug reports, and other messages you send to us.
  • Account or sign-in information if you choose to use authentication features.

2.2 Information created through app use

  • Generated images, saved designs, app settings, local history, and generation metadata.
  • Images uploaded for processing and output files returned by generation services.
  • Subscription status, trial eligibility, restore state, and entitlement information provided by Apple or App Store systems.

2.3 Information collected automatically

  • Device and technical information such as app version, device model, operating system version, language, region, timestamps, diagnostics, and crash data.
  • Usage information such as launches, screen views, feature interactions, and performance events.
  • Security and abuse-prevention signals needed to protect the app and services.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate AI Deco and provide image generation, redesign, editing, saving, restore, and subscription features.
  • To upload selected images, send generation requests, receive outputs, and return results to you.
  • To store app data locally on your device and keep the app experience working correctly.
  • To validate purchases, manage premium access, and support subscription restoration.
  • To monitor reliability, diagnose crashes, investigate bugs, prevent abuse, and improve the app.
  • To respond to support requests and comply with legal obligations.

4. Photos, Prompts, and AI Processing

AI Deco uses artificial intelligence and cloud processing to generate interior design images. When you request generation, your selected images, prompts, style settings, and related parameters may be transmitted to cloud providers and model infrastructure needed to perform the requested task.

Please do not submit images or prompts containing highly sensitive personal information, confidential material, or content you are not comfortable sending to external processing providers.

We do not claim ownership of your submitted images or generated outputs. Processing providers may handle submitted content under their own retention, security, and service practices.

5. Third-Party Services

AI Deco may use third-party services for hosting, image storage, AI processing, analytics, diagnostics, authentication, subscriptions, and platform features. These providers may process data as needed to deliver the app functionality you request.

Payments and App Store subscriptions are handled by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card number.

6. App Permissions

AI Deco may request access to device features such as camera, photo library, local storage, notifications, and authentication services. You can manage permissions in iOS Settings. If permissions are denied or revoked, some features may not work.

7. Local Storage and Saved Results

The app may store settings, saved images, generated results, and local records on your device. Deleting the app, clearing local data, or removing saved items may delete locally stored content. Some data already processed by platform or third-party services may be retained according to their practices.

8. How We Share Information

  • With cloud, storage, AI, analytics, diagnostics, security, and infrastructure providers needed to operate AI Deco.
  • With Apple and App Store-related services for purchases, subscriptions, permissions, diagnostics, and platform functionality.
  • With authorities, regulators, or legal advisors when required by law or necessary to protect rights and safety.
  • With a successor or relevant party in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or part of the business.

We do not sell personal information for money.

9. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain subscriptions, support security, troubleshoot issues, comply with law, and meet legitimate operational needs. Retention periods vary depending on the data type and provider.

10. International Processing

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than where you live. Data protection laws in those countries may differ from your local laws.

11. Your Rights and Choices

  • Manage camera, photo library, notification, and other permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Manage subscriptions and purchases through your Apple Account.
  • Delete local app data by using app controls where available or by deleting the app.
  • Contact us about privacy requests using the email below.

12. Children’s Privacy

AI Deco is not directed to children under 13, or under the minimum age required by local law for digital consent. If you believe a child provided personal data unlawfully, contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

13. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information. No internet transmission, cloud service, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we may provide notice through the app, App Store materials, website, or another reasonable method.
